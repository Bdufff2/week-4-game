Crystals Collector

Instructions:

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points total score.

  • You win the game by matching your total score to ransdom number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

  • Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
